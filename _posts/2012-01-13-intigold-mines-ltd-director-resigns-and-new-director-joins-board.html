News Release 2012-01

January 13, 2012

Vancouver, B.C. January 13, 2012 Lori McClenahan, President of Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD – TSX:V) (“Intigold” or the “Company”) announces that Duncan Bain resigned as a director of the Company on January 11, 2012.
The Company is pleased to announce that Lloyd C. Brewer has been elected to Intigold’s board of directors effective today. The current directors of the Company are as follows:

Lori McClenahan
Murry Braucht
Tina Whyte
Lloyd Brewer

Lloyd Brewer brings to Intigold more than 30 years of international experience in the mineral exploration business.

In announcing the appointment of Mr. Brewer, Lori McClenahan, President of the Company states “as we continue our growth, we are pleased to have Lloyd Brewer’s extensive experience in the mineral exploration business join our board”.
INTIGOLD MINES LTD.


(signed “Lori McClenahan”)

Lori McClenahan
President
