News Release 2012-10

June 14, 2012

Vancouver, BC, June 14, 2012, Lori McClenahan, President of Intigold Mines Ltd. (“the “Company”), announces today that the acquisition of a 51% interest in TTAGIT Social Networks Inc. (“TTAGIT“) has been conditionally accepted by the TSX Venture Exchange (the “Exchange”).

The acquisition of a 51% interest in TTAGIT (the “Transaction”) has been conditionally accepted by the Exchange subject to:

1. the Company filing a filing statement with the Exchange with respect to the Transaction;

2. the Company providing audited financial statements of TTAGIT (to be incorporated into the filing statement referred to under item 1);

3. the Company providing a Business Plan of TTAGIT (to be incorporated into the filing statement referred to under item 1);

4. the Company providing an updated copy of the principal agreement governing the Transaction to the Exchange; and

5. shareholder approval of the Transaction.
