Intigold Mines Ltd. – Launches New Corporate Web-Site

Vancouver, B.C. September 25, 2011 Lori McClenahan, President, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX:V) (the “Company or Intigold”) is pleased to announce the new corporate web site has been launched. The URL for the Company’s web site is www.intigold.com.

The new web site has a user friendly configuration to access information on Intigold that allows investors and interested parties to easily view the Company’s corporate and property information. Technical reports on our projects are also available on the site.
