INTIGOLD MINES LTD. - Engages Susan Blond, Inc. as Public Relations Representative

Vancouver, B.C. October 7, 2012 Lori McClenahan, President of Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD – TSX:V) (“Intigold” or the “Company”) announces that the Company has engaged Susan Blond, Inc. ("SBI"), as public relations representative for Intigold Mines Ltd.

The Company has engaged SBI:  coordinating media coverage relating to Intigold Mines Ltd. (including all forms of print media, television and internet) within the United States of America; supervising the preparation and issuance of press releases, pitch letters and media alerts; providing general media interview preparation; and coordinating and supervising certain other activities which the Company and Susan Blond, Inc. deem appropriate.

Susan Blond, Inc. is a publicity agency with 25 years of bringing clients to another level through targeted media campaigns and strategic counsel. We are a firm with storied leadership that took its roots in the music community and grew to serve core industries that also include nightlife and hospitality, non-profit organizations, fashion and beauty, personalities and executives, businesses, products and services, theater and fine arts, crisis management and special events. We are a full-service organization handling our clients’ traditional and digital media needs, on a regional and national scale, working above and beyond the standard campaign to draw exposure to all aspects of an individual’s personality.
