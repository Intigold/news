NEWS RELEASE

Intigold Mines Ltd. - TTAGIT 

Vancouver, BC, April 22, 2013, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD – TSX.V and IDMNF – OTC QX) has received an update from TTAGIT Social Networks Inc. ("TTAGIT").  TTAGIT has signed a Non-Disclosure Agreement with Microsoft Corporation and its affiliates to allow disclosure of confidential information with respect to TTAGIT technologies.

Developed and owned 51% by Intigold Mines Ltd., TTAGIT is a revolutionary online tool that lets you comment on everything on the internet from your browser without having to login to several accounts or register with a number of other websites.  Designed to “tag” the moment, TTAGIT is the new face of commenting and social networking.

TTAGIT, the first open-forum discussion social network and comment management system that permits users to comment on all URLs with one login and allows you to talk over any website without leaving the page.  TTAGIT is a commenting and social networking tool where members can engage in conversations and participate in debates on sites and portals that currently do not allow live streaming.  For more information please visit: http://ttagit.com/

Lori McClenahan, President and CEO of Intigold Mines Ltd., has been appointed a director of TTAGIT as a representative for Intigold's investment of a 51% interest in TTAGIT.
