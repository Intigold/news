Intigold Mines Ltd. – Resignation of Director and Corporate Secretary 

Vancouver, BC, June 24, 2013, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD – TSX.V and IDMNF – OTC QX) announces that the Board of Directors of the Company has received and accepted the resignation of Tina Whyte as a Director and the Corporate Secretary of the Company.  St. Elias would like to thank Ms. Whyte for her service and contribution to the Company and wish her every success in the future.
