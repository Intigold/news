Intigold Mines Ltd. - TTAGIT Update

Vancouver, BC, July 30, 2013, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX.V and IDMNF - OTC QX) has received the following update from TTAGIT Social Networks Inc. ("TTAGIT"). Intigold owns a 51% interest in TTAGIT.

"TTAGIT's Next Step – Monetization

TTAGIT's planned next step is to monetize its competitive advantage of providing the ability to comment over any URL or website using live feed. TTAGIT intends to monetize this advantage utilizing strategic alliances with existing businesses on the internet. For example:

  •  with online shopping networks where buyers would have the ability to shop and make a decision while reading comments from previous purchasers of a product; and
  •  with online trading companies where clients would have the ability to trade online while reading the latest internet news and relevant topics.

TTAGIT Developing Phatpen

TTAGIT is presently developing "Phatpen", an application being built over top of the TTAGIT engine which will allow users to make stock trades right over top of a web page with a browser extension or smartphone. Our goal with Phatpen is to enable users to connect to their trading accounts to make web surfing and trading seamless.

Once completed, Phatpen will also let you automate trades, allowing you to set prices and amounts to automatically buy or sell at the given prices you want. A live feed will keep you updated on the markets you want to watch, never missing a news release. Using the TTAGIT engine, users will be able to engage in discussions about markets on the bullboards right over top of any webpage.

Phatpen will be used on Google Chrome, Firefox, IOS or Android."
