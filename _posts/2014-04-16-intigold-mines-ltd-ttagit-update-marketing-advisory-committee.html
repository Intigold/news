NEWS RELEASE

VANCOUVER, BC / April 16, 2014 / Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) has received the following update from TTAGIT Social Networks Inc. ("TTAGIT"). Intigold owns a 51% interest in TTAGIT.

TTAGIT Forms Marketing Advisory Committee

TTAGIT has formed a Marketing Advisory Committee ("MAC") consisting of Lisa Tenner and Eddie Wenrick. The MAC is charged with branding TTAGIT and de2our, identifying corporate opportunities and determining innovative and effective marketing to increase the visibility and awareness of TTAGIT and de2our.

Lisa Tenner, President and founder of Tenner and Associates, Inc., is considered a visionary among her peers and industry leaders. Tenner and Associates, Inc. is a Las Vegas-based company focused on branding and marketing in the corporate world as well as event productions and sponsorships in the entertainment, sports, convention and other industries.

Ms. Tenner is on the advisory board of the American Gaming Association. Additionally Ms. Tenner was named "Event Producer of the Year" for her work in live performance production and representation of artists and celebrities.

Eddie Wenrick's career in music and entertainment has encompassed the roles of record label executive, artist manager, concert promoter, music magazine publisher, executive producer, and radio broadcasting executive. He has spent the last 40 plus years traversing virtually every medium, including music, print and digital media, film, television and sports.
