NEWS RELEASE

Vancouver, BC,  July 29, 2014,  Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) announced today that it has recieved the following update from TTAGIT Social Networks Inc. ("TTAGIT"). Intigold owns a 51% interest in TTAGIT.

Intigold Mines Ltd. – TTAGIT Development Plan - Update

TTAGIT Development Plan -Update

TTAGIT Social Networks would like to announce that the TTAGIT Application now works on Firefox desktop and is in the Queue waiting to be reviewed at the Firefox add-ons store. Until then you can find it at https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/ttagit/ to try it out. The Firefox add-on is slightly different from its Google Chrome plugin in that it works as a sidebar rather than a popup. The benefit of TTAGIT working as a sidebar instead of a popup is that the application remains open while switching between browser tabs while changing the content within the application.

This latest advancement is part of TTAGIT's development plan. For further details on the development plan, please refer to news release dates June 12, 2014.
