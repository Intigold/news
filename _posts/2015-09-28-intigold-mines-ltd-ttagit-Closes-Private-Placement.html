NEWS RELEASE

Vancouver, BC, September 28, 2015, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) announces that pursuant to its news release dated September 25, 2015 and subject to regulatory approval, the Company has closed the private placement of 2,000,000 units at a price of $0.15 per unit raising gross proceeds of $300,000. Each unit will consist of one common share and one common share purchase warrant (a "Warrant"). Each Warrant will entitle the holder to purchase one additional common share at a price of $0.20 per share for a period of 24 months from closing. The proceeds will be used for general working capital. The Company will pay finder's fees of up to 10% of the gross proceeds raised, payable in cash or shares or a combination thereof.

For information on Intigold and its projects, please visit us at www.intigold.com or call 604-669-4677 or toll free at 1-888-895-5522.
