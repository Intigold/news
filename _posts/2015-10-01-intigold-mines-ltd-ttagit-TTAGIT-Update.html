NEWS RELEASE

Vancouver, BC, October 01, 2015, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) announced that it has received the following update from TTAGIT Social Networks Inc. ("TTAGIT"). Intigold owns a 51% interest in TTAGIT.

TTAGIT has launched a new website that contains updates made to the TTAGIT system. TTAGIT has also made a embeddable version that users can embed on their own websites to track social activity and sentiments. By adding the embeddable version, TTAGIT has entered into the realm that applications such as Intense Debate, Disqus, Livefyre, and Facebook comments dominate

The TTAGIT embed will have an embeddable button that can be placed on webpages to allow users to use TTAGIT in an iframe that opens when the TTAGIT button is clicked. Because the button is an iframe, it is also unique from any other Social Button that shares. The TTAGIT button is not about sharing but about seeing what has been shared on the link. The TTAGIT button also acts as a unique way to comment and interact with people who are related to the content of the link semantically. TTAGIT is the ultimate sharing button as it also displays what has been shared on the link by Twitter or Facebook publicly.

Along with these updates TTAGIT has rebranded with a new logo and a new look. In addition, the other extensions are being rebranded as well

TTAGIT - Technology

TTAGIT is a proprietary technology which allows Internet users to comment, talk and respond over any website (URL) using live feed. By using TTAGIT, users can stay on a webpage and post to their favorite social networks, without ever going to different website's to view or post comments. This is what gives TTAGIT its competitive advantage.
