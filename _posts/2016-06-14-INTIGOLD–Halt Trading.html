INTIGOLD – Halt Trading

Vancouver, BC, June 14, 2016, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) (OTC:IDMNF, - News) announces that it has been halted by IIROC (Market Surveillance) as it does not meet director requirements for a public company. The Company will remain halted until a new director is appointed.
