Intigold Mines Ltd. - Appoints New Director

Vancouver, BC, June 30, 2016, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) (OTC:IDMNF, - News) announced today the appointment of Greg Kallal as a Director of the Company.

Mr. Kallal joined Intigold as a Director of the Company on June 29, 2016, bringing over 30 years of business and corporate experience to the Company from his background in the car sales and dealership industries. Mr. Kallal is a director of the Alberta Dealers Association and a director of the local golf course in the area he resides (both non-public companies).
