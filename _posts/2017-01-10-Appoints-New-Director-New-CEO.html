INTIGOLD – TTAGIT Update

Vancouver, BC, January 10, 2017, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) (OTC:IDMNF, - News) announced today the appointment of Leigh Akin as a Director of the Company and Paul Gray as CEO of the Company.

Leigh K Akin has been appointed as a Director of the Company. Mr. Akin is a technology innovator and is the creator of the TTAGIT technology.

The Board of Directors of Intigold consists of Paul Gray, Greg Kallal and Leigh Akin.

In addition, Paul Gray, who has been a Director of Intigold since 2012, has been appointed as the CEO of the Company. Mr. Gray holds a Bachelor of Science (Honours) degree from Dalhousie University and is a member in good standing with the Association of Professional Engineers and Geoscientists of British Columbia. Mr. Gray has extensive public company experience.
