Intigold Mines Ltd. – Greg Kallal resigns as Director of the Company

Vancouver, BC, March 27, 2017, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) (OTC:IDMNF, - News) announces that effective March 27, 2017, Greg Kallal has resigned as Director of the Company.

The remaining Directors on the board of the “Company” are Paul Gray (President and CEO), Peter Haukedal (Director), and Leigh Akin (Director).

Greg has decided to step down for personal reasons. We want to thank Greg for his skill, knowledge and financial backing in helping to guide the company since early July 2016. We wish him the best in his future endeavours.

For information on Intigold and its projects, please visit us at www.intigold.com or call 604-669-4677 or toll free at 1-888-895-5522.
