Intigold Mines Ltd. – Leigh Akin and Paul Gray resign as Directors of the Company

Vancouver, BC, April 10, 2017, Intigold Mines Ltd. (IGD - TSX-V) (OTC:IDMNF, - News) announces that effective April 8, 2017, Leigh Akin and Paul Gray has resigned as Director of the Company.

The remaining Director on the board of the “Company” is Peter Haukedal.
